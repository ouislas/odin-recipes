Veggie omelette

Description

A veggie omelette is a nutritious, no-fuss breakfast option that can be prepared in mere minutes and always turns out great! With a little practice, you'll find that it's hard to go wrong with this classic staple.

Ingredients

Everything you'll need for this no frills recipe:

  • 3 grade-A eggs
  • 1/2 tomato, diced
  • 1 handful of spinach
  • 10g sliced mushroom of your choice
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Vegetable oil or butter

Instructions

  1. Use the vegetable oil to lubricate a saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Crack your eggs into a bowl and stir until liquified, season with salt and pepper.
  3. Add your sliced tomatoes, mushrooms, and spinach into the egg mix.
  4. Add the concoction into the now heated saucepan.
  5. Allow the omellete to cook until the sides harden.
  6. Once the sides have hardened, fold omlette in half and cook for two minutes.
  7. Flip and cook for two minutes.
  8. Turn off stove heat and allow to sit for three minutes.
