Turkey sandwich

Description

A reliable easy to prepare dish that works great when time is of the essence. Pair with your favorite chips and you're all set!

Ingredients

  • Two slices of sandwich bread
  • Four slices of turkey
  • Two slices of cheese, your choice
  • Mayo
  • Honey mustard
  • Spinach
  • Last but not least, chips!

Instructions

  1. Spread mayo and honeymustard over each slice of bread, bake until crispy.
  2. Remove bread from heat, top with cheese, turkey, and spinach.
  3. Close the sandwich and cut in half diagonally.
  4. Serve on plate with chips. Done.
