Classic spaghetti

Description

Loved everywhere, this classic dish is easy to make, cost-effective, unpretensious, and most of all, delicous.

Ingredients

All you'll need to whip this up in no time are the following simple ingredients:

  • Spaghetti noodles, any brand
  • Canned tomato sauce.
  • Ground beef
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Basil

Instructions

  1. Bring a pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
  2. Dump your portion of spaghetti into the boiling water, allow them to cook for 9 minutes.
  3. Once fully cooked, strain noodles and discard the water.
  4. In separate sauce pan, cook ground beef over high heat for 10 minutes.
  5. Once beef is cooked, mix tomato sauce into sauce pan and stir occasionally over 5 minutes.
  6. Mix meat sauce mixture into the spaghetti and stil until well mixed.
  7. Done. It is now ready to serve.
